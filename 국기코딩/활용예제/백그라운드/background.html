IDEAS FOR BETTER INDOOR AGRICULTURE
  • Don't leave the hose running.
  • In a small apartment, coffee grounds make better planting material than chicken manure.
  • Water-proof your planters.
  • Kudzu is to be avoided.
  • Use electric, not gas powered mowers for indoor sod.
  • Steer clear of plants with deep roots.
